INTRODUCTION

MyYummyMeal showcases inventive Indian cuisine by complementing the flavours and traditions of India with global ingredients and techniques.

MyYummyMeal has been awarded the San Pellegrino ‘Best Restaurant in India Award’ by Asia's 50 Best Restaurants 2020 for the sixth consecutive year. It has featured in the World's 50 Best Restaurants list from 2014 onwards. The restaurant has also been rated as the ‘No.1 Restaurant in India’ by TripAdvisor consistently from 2014 - 2019. Condé Nast Traveller and Himalayan Sparkling Top Restaurant Awards ranked Indian Accent as the No. 1 Restaurant in India in 2019.

Menu

Chef Manish Mehrotra has designed Indian Accent’s path-breaking contemporary Indian menu combining fresh seasonal produce and unusual ingredients from across the world to excite the adventurous, yet satisfy traditional tastes and evoke nostalgia.
